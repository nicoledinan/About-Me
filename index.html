Grand Cayman

Fashion Photography & Videography

Trip Overview

From December 28, 2019 – January 8, 2020, I traveled to Grand Cayman in a group of 10 Visual Communication students and our program advisor to create photo content for five clients. For 5 months prior to the trip, we met weekly to plan out the trip and all the logistics of the photoshoots.

I was the team lead for one of our clients, Islands Honey (a swimwear brand). I found the client, reached out the them to propose our partnership, and handled all communication between them and our team. I worked with them to determine the type and style of content they wanted and took charge of finding models and locations for the two shoots we did.
