Trip Overview

From December 28, 2019 – January 8, 2020, I traveled to Grand Cayman to create photo content for five clients with a group of 9 Commercial Photography students. For 5 months prior to the trip, we met weekly to plan out the trip and all the logistics of the photoshoots.

I was the team lead for one of our clients, Islands Honey (a swimwear brand). As the team lead, I found the client, reached out the them to propose our partnership, and handled all communication between our teams. I worked with the client to determine the type and style of content they wanted and took charge of finding models and locations for the two shoots we did for this client.
